Brain Test (罗码术) is a fully offline Android brain test app with 58 testing tools across 6 categories:

🧠 Neuro Reaction - APM test, CPS click test, reaction speed, choice reaction, timing challenge, gaming talent
🧩 Cognitive Thinking - Memory, Schulte grid, Stroop test, IQ test, logic reasoning, focus training and 13 more
🪞 Personality Mirror - MBTI, Big Five, Enneagram, EQ, Holland codes and 12 more
📚 Knowledge Trivia - Idioms, poetry, science, history, geography, English vocabulary and 12 more
👁️ Sensory Challenge - Vision, hearing, pitch, rhythm, color sensitivity, spot the difference and 8 more
🎮 Fun Playground - Minesweeper, number guess, chase button, don't press it, circle challenge and 7 more

Features:
• Fully offline - no internet permission required
• Local data storage - your privacy is protected
• Beautiful Material3 UI design
• Haptic feedback for enhanced interaction
• One-tap share your results
• Track your best scores and progress

Each test takes only 10 seconds to 3 minutes - perfect for quick brain training anytime, anywhere.
